# SIEM Tools — Topic Breakdown

**Splunk** – one of the most widely used commercial SIEMs, known for powerful search/query language (SPL) and dashboards; common in large enterprises but expensive at scale.

**Wazuh** – free, open-source SIEM/XDR built on top of the Elastic Stack, popular for learning and smaller deployments since it's free and has a large community.

**ELK Stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana)** – not a dedicated SIEM out of the box, but a flexible log storage/search/visualization stack that many teams (and tools like Wazuh) build SIEM capability on top of.

**Security Onion** – a free, Linux-based platform bundling multiple security tools (including SIEM, IDS, and network monitoring) together, popular for home labs and small teams wanting an all-in-one setup.

**QRadar (IBM)** – another major commercial SIEM, strong in large enterprise and government environments, known for deep correlation rules and compliance reporting features.
