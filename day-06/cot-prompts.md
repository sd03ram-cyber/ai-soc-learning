# 5 Chain-of-Thought Prompts

1. "Analyze this login log step by step and identify suspicious activity: 'User admin: failed login x4, then success, from IP outside usual geo, at 3 AM.' Think through each factor before concluding."

2. "A server's CPU usage jumped from 10% to 95% right after a scheduled task ran. Let's think step by step about whether this is normal load or something malicious."

3. "This firewall log shows outbound connections to 3 different IPs on port 4444 within 1 minute of a suspicious email being opened. Reason step by step about what likely happened."

4. "An employee downloaded 500 files from a shared drive at 11 PM on a weekend, then the account was disabled the next morning. Walk through step by step whether this looks like insider threat or a legitimate reason."

5. "A vulnerability scan shows 3 critical CVEs on a public-facing server. Think step by step about which one to patch first and why, considering exploitability and exposure."
