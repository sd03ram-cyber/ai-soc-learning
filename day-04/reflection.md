# Day 4 – Reflection

**AI vs ML vs LLM, in my own words:**
- AI is the overall goal — machines doing tasks that need intelligence.
- ML is one way to achieve AI — learning patterns from data instead of hardcoded rules.
- LLM is a specific type of ML/DL model, focused on language, trained on huge amounts of text to predict and generate words.

**Why prompt quality matters:**
A vague prompt gets a vague or generic answer. Adding clear context, a specific instruction, and the exact output format needed cuts down on back-and-forth and reduces the chance of the model guessing wrong or hallucinating — especially important when using AI to help with security work where accuracy matters.
